My Wellness Journey

From struggling with energy and focus to discovering the transformative power of gut health and proper hydration.

The Beginning

My name is Logen Dickey, and when covid hit I gained over 60 pounds, lost a lot of my muscle, and struggled with energy levels and mental motivation. I had the frustrating cycle of high and low energy that went on for days, weeks, and months. This is also how I felt mentally. I never quite knew if I could stay consistent with anything and take back control of my life. I would be super productive for a few weeks, then I would fall off my high horse and be back to my old habits for a few months. It was a terrible cycle that I couldn't break. I tried countless supplements and diets, but nothing seemed to provide the lasting change I was looking for.

"From my first keto diet back in early 2022 to intermittent fasting, consistent exercise, and hard diet changes, I just couldn't keep my physical or mental motivation up."
Before

The Discovery

Everything changed when I realized the crucial connection that my energy and mental levels were directly related to my gut health and hydration. It took a lot of trial and error, but I finally found a way to stay consistent with my health. For the first time in my life really, I was able to stay consistent on everything from my diet, to my exercise, to my supplements. My energy levels were rising, but not just one day or one week, it was every single day. My poor gut health was causing my mental state to be all over the place. Once I realized this, I knew it was time to make a change.

  • Improved mental clarity and focus
  • Consistent energy throughout the day
  • Enhanced workout performance
The Turning Point

Today

Now, I'm experiencing levels of energy and mental clarity that I haven't felt since my youth. Through proper gut health and hydration, I've unlocked a new chapter in my wellness journey. Of course, my mental state and clarity has been amazing as well. The best part? I'm only just getting started! This feeling is something I want to share with the world.

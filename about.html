The Wellness Warriors Story

From a personal transformation to building a community dedicated to helping others discover the power of gut health and proper hydration.

The Beginning

My name is Logen Dickey, and when COVID hit, I gained over 60 pounds, lost significant muscle mass, and struggled with fluctuating energy levels and mental motivation. I experienced the frustrating cycle of high and low energy that persisted for days, weeks, and months. This pattern affected me both physically and mentally. I never quite knew if I could maintain consistency with anything or take back control of my life. I would be super productive for a few weeks, then fall back into old habits for months. It was a challenging cycle that seemed impossible to break. Despite trying countless supplements and diets, nothing provided the lasting change I was seeking.

"From my first keto diet back in early 2022 to intermittent fasting, consistent exercise, and hard diet changes, I just couldn't keep my physical or mental motivation up."
Before

The Discovery

Everything changed when I discovered the crucial connection between my energy levels, mental clarity, and gut health combined with proper hydration. Through careful experimentation and research, I finally found a sustainable approach to wellness. For the first time in my life, I maintained consistency in everything from diet to exercise and supplementation. My energy levels weren't just improving for a day or a week—they were consistently high every single day. I realized that my poor gut health had been affecting my mental state significantly. Once I understood this connection, I knew I had found the key to lasting change.

  • Improved mental clarity and focus
  • Consistent energy throughout the day
  • Enhanced workout performance
The Turning Point
Family Support

The Power of Support

One of the most significant turning points in my journey was when my father joined me on this path. Having family support not only strengthened my resolve but also validated that these changes could benefit anyone, regardless of age or background. Together, we've discovered that proper gut health and hydration can transform lives, and we're committed to sharing this knowledge with others.

"Seeing my father experience the same transformative benefits I had discovered made me realize this wasn't just about personal wellness anymore—it was about creating a community where everyone could thrive together."

Today, we share the same mission: helping others discover their path to wellness through proper gut health and hydration. His journey alongside mine has become a testament to the power of community and support in achieving lasting wellness.

Today

Now, I'm experiencing levels of energy and mental clarity that I haven't felt since my youth. Through proper gut health and hydration, I've unlocked a new chapter in my wellness journey. My mental state and clarity have been consistently amazing, and the best part is that I'm only just getting started! This transformation is something I want to share with others who are facing similar struggles.
